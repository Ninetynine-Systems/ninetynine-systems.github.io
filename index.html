Gatekeeper.

Nothing happens until someone says yes.

Universal approval infrastructure for people, AI agents, and machines. It holds risky actions — deploys, payments, database changes, an agent’s next move — until the right person approves, then keeps a signed, verifiable record of who said yes, when, and why.
